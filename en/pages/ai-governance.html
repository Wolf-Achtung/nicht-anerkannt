AI Governance

Our AI features support thinking and creativity. They do not make decisions about you.

Purpose

What We Use AI For

  • Creative reformulations in the Manifesto Remixer.
  • Generation of thinking prompts and questions.
  • Interactive learning and reflection formats.

The tools serve as sparring partners. Responsibility for interpretation and action remains with the human.

Risk

Classification Under the EU AI Act

The features deployed are classified as low risk: no biometric identification, no credit scoring, no automated decisions with legal effect.

Transparency and Control

  • We clearly label AI-powered features.
  • No sensitive personal data is processed.
  • Curated published content is subject to human moderation.
