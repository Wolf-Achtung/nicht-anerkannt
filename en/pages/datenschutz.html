Privacy Policy

We operate data-minimally: personal inputs in the tools remain locally in your browser and are not stored on any server.

Responsibility

Responsible Entity

What Data We Process

  • Technical access data in anonymized form (e.g., timestamp, page visited), insofar as necessary for secure operation.
  • Local usage data such as thinking profile score, name and city in the stamp generator are stored in the browser and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • No tracking cookies and no profiling for advertising purposes.
Tools

AI-Powered Features

Features such as the Remixer, Thinking Challenge Generator, and Quiz serve reflection and creative engagement. The outputs involve no automated decisions about individuals and no assessment of sensitive characteristics.

Thinking Challenge of the Day

The Thinking Challenge of the Day is loaded via an API and may optionally be AI-generated. If the service is unavailable, a local archive question is displayed. Answers to thinking challenges remain exclusively in your browser (localStorage) and are not transmitted to our servers.

You can delete the Thinking Challenge archive at any time directly on the homepage via the "Delete Thinking Challenge Archive" button.

Storage Duration and Deletion

Locally stored data can be reset at any time within the respective tool (e.g., via the reset in the Thinking Profile) or removed by clearing your browser data.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
