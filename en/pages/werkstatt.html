AI as Sparring Partner / Thinking Tools / Judgment Training

AI Workshop

Ten AI-powered tools that challenge your thinking – not replace it. AI here is a sparring partner, counter-voice, and stress test.

Contradiction

Contradiction Salon

State a thesis – the AI generates three well-founded counter-positions from different perspectives. No straw man, but a real challenge.

Thinking Challenge

Thinking Challenge Generator

Enter a current topic – the AI produces a structured thinking challenge in the Atelier's style: problem statement, false alternative, radical middle, open questions.

Judgment

Judgment Training

A real dilemma – no right or wrong. Make your judgment, and the AI reveals your blind spots, your strengths, and pushes your thinking further.

Wicked Problem

Wicked Problem Workshop

Choose a complex problem and work through five thinking steps: disciplines, stakeholders, contradictions, options for action, judgment. In the end, you have your personal working paper.

Stress Test

Text Stress Test

Submit your own text – the AI doesn't check grammar, but thinking quality. Where is assertion without reasoning? Where is contradiction missing? Where is opinion disguised as judgment?

Translator

Manifesto Translator

Not Google Translate. A cultural adaptation – how does "Radical Middle" sound in Japanese? What is the Arabic equivalent of "judgment"?

Perspective

Perspective-Shifting Machine

Enter your position and choose a perspective – the AI reformulates your position empathetically and convincingly from the chosen viewpoint. No straw man, but genuine understanding.

Counter-speech

AI Counter-Speech on News Articles

Paste a news article or opinion piece – the AI delivers the counter-position, the unasked question, the unspoken assumptions, and the missing voice.

Argument Map

Argument Map

Enter a thesis – the AI creates a visual argument map with pro and contra branches, justifications, objections, and a synthesis.

Silence

Silence Mode: AI Only Asks Questions

No advice, no opinion, no answers. In Silence Mode, the AI exclusively asks questions – purely Socratic. Write what's on your mind and let yourself be guided only through questions.
